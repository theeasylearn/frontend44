F.A.Q.

01. What types of plants do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of indoor, outdoor, medicinal, and ornamental plants, along with seasonal flowers and fruit plants.

02. Do you provide home delivery?

Yes, we offer home delivery services within Bhavnagar. Contact us for more details on delivery charges and availability.

03. Do you provide gardening services?

Yes, we offer complete gardening services, including landscaping, plant maintenance, and garden setup for homes and offices.

04. How can I take care of my plants?

We provide plant care tips, including watering schedules, sunlight requirements, and pest control solutions. Visit our nursery for expert advice.

05. Do you sell fertilizers and gardening tools?

Yes, we have a range of organic and chemical fertilizers, along with high-quality gardening tools to help you maintain your plants efficiently.

06. Do you provide customized landscaping solutions?

Yes, we offer customized landscaping solutions for homes, offices, and commercial spaces, tailored to your needs and preferences.

07. Do you have seasonal plants available?

Yes, we offer a variety of seasonal plants and flowers throughout the year. Visit us to explore our latest collection.

08. Can you help with pest control for plants?

Yes, we provide expert advice and organic as well as chemical pest control solutions to keep your plants healthy.

09. Do you offer bulk plant supply for events and businesses?

Yes, we supply plants in bulk for events, corporate gifting, and commercial spaces. Contact us for customized orders.

10. Where is Narmada Nursery located?

Narmada Nursery is located at 105, Eva Surbhi, Opp. Aksharwadi Temple, Waghawadi Road, Bhavnagar, Gujarat. Visit us for all your gardening needs.
